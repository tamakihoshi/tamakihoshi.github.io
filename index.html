I am a Freelance Writer in pursuit to pen the quiet beauty of Japan's culture and forgotten histories. Raised in the United States and returned to Japan, I studied Japanese history and culture at Waseda University, immersing myself in Okinawa's local narratives. My work encompasses academic research, cultural commentary, and travel narratives, each piece an attempt to capture the fleeting beauty and profound stories of Japan.
