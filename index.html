星たまき

I am a writer and scholar devoted to capturing untold stories in Japan. Raised in the United States, I returned to Tokyo to study Japanese history and society at Waseda University, where I immersed myself in communities across the country—from the wartime archives of Okinawa and the legacy of premodern samurai in Fukushima to the contemporary landscapes of media and pageantry in Tokyo. My work spans academic research, cultural commentary, and travel writing, all driven by a mission to illuminate lost voices, past and present.
